Practical Information

What type of electrical outlets are used?

Power outlets on Taniti are 120 volts‚Äîthe same standard used in the United States.

What currency is used on Taniti?

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar. Many businesses also accept euros and yen. Several banks can exchange currency, and many businesses accept major credit cards.

What language is spoken?

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. Very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by older residents.

Local Rules & Holidays

When can alcohol be purchased?

Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between midnight and 9:00 a.m.

What is the drinking age?

The drinking age on Taniti is 18. The law exists, but it is not strictly enforced.

Are attractions open during holidays?

Taniti observes many national holidays. Some tourist attractions and restaurants may be closed on holidays, so visitors should plan accordingly.

Health & Safety

Is Taniti safe?

Violent crime is very rare on Taniti. As tourism has increased, there have been more reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes‚Äîso standard travel precautions are recommended.

What medical care is available?

Taniti has one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees.
