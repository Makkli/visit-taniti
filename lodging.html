Where to Stay

Accommodations for every budget and style

Find Your Perfect Stay

Taniti offers a range of accommodations from luxury resorts to budget-friendly hostels

Taniti Resort (Four-Star)

Our premier oceanfront resort offers luxury accommodations with stunning views, world-class amenities, and exceptional service.

  • Oceanfront rooms and suites available
  • Full-service spa and wellness center
  • Multiple on-site restaurants and bars
  • Pool, fitness center, and beach access
  • Concierge services for activities and tours

Price Range: $$$$ (Premium)

Hotels

Taniti has several family-owned hotels throughout the island, offering comfortable rooms and convenient locations near attractions.

  • Various locations in Taniti City and coastal areas
  • Air-conditioned rooms with modern amenities
  • On-site or nearby dining options
  • Friendly, personalized service
  • Many within walking distance of beaches

Price Range: $$ - $$$ (Moderate)

Bed & Breakfasts

Experience authentic Tanitian hospitality at one of our charming bed and breakfasts. Perfect for travelers seeking a more personal touch.

  • Locally-owned and operated
  • Homemade breakfast included daily
  • Unique, individually decorated rooms
  • Local tips and recommendations from hosts
  • Often located in scenic, quiet areas

Price Range: $$ (Moderate)

Hostel

Budget-conscious travelers can stay at our hostel near Merriton Landing, offering clean, affordable accommodations in a social atmosphere.

  • Dormitory-style and private rooms available
  • Shared kitchen facilities
  • Common areas for meeting other travelers
  • Walking distance to restaurants and nightlife
  • Ideal for backpackers and solo travelers

Price Range: $ (Budget)

Booking Tips

Peak Season: December through February sees the highest visitor numbers. Book at least 2-3 months in advance for these dates.

Best Value: Visit during shoulder season (March-May or September-November) for lower rates and fewer crowds.

Note: Taniti's lodging capacity is expanding! New hotels and vacation rentals are currently under construction to meet growing demand.
