Things to Do in Taniti

Adventures for every type of traveler

Beaches & Water Activities

Crystal-clear waters and pristine white sand beaches await

White Sand Beaches

Taniti's beaches are famous for their powdery white sand and calm, crystal-clear waters. Perfect for families and relaxation.

  • Multiple beach locations around the island
  • Safe swimming areas with lifeguards
  • Beach equipment rentals available
  • Sunset viewing spots on the west coast

Snorkeling & Scuba Diving

Explore vibrant coral reefs teeming with tropical fish. Our waters offer visibility of up to 100 feet on clear days.

  • Guided snorkeling tours available daily
  • Scuba certification courses offered
  • Equipment rental from local shops
  • Night diving experiences for certified divers

Fishing & Boat Tours

Join local fishermen for an authentic experience or charter a boat for deep-sea fishing adventures.

  • Half-day and full-day fishing charters
  • Glass-bottom boat tours
  • Sunset sailing cruises
  • Whale watching (seasonal)

Nature & Adventure

From volcanic peaks to lush rainforests

Volcano Tours

Taniti is home to an active volcano that last erupted over 200 years ago. Today, it offers spectacular hiking and sightseeing opportunities.

  • Guided hiking tours to the crater rim
  • Helicopter tours for aerial views
  • Visit the volcanic hot springs
  • Photography tours at sunrise/sunset

Rainforest Adventures

Our tropical rainforest covers much of the island's interior, featuring stunning waterfalls, exotic wildlife, and ancient trees.

  • Guided nature hikes (various difficulty levels)
  • Waterfall swimming excursions
  • Bird watching tours
  • Canopy walkways and observation platforms

Zip-lining

Experience the thrill of soaring through the rainforest canopy on our professionally designed zipline course.

  • Multiple zipline courses available
  • Suitable for ages 8 and up
  • All safety equipment provided
  • Trained guides accompany all tours

Entertainment & Culture

Experience the heart and soul of Taniti

Nightlife & Entertainment

Taniti City's Merriton Landing area comes alive after dark with restaurants, bars, and live entertainment.

  • Microbrewery with local craft beers
  • Dance club (open Thursday-Saturday)
  • Live music at various venues
  • Casino gaming available

Cultural Experiences

Learn about Taniti's rich history and vibrant culture through museums, galleries, and local events.

  • Taniti History Museum
  • Local art galleries
  • Traditional craft demonstrations
  • Cultural festivals (check seasonal calendar)

Pro Tip

Book popular activities like volcano tours and zipline adventures in advance, especially during peak tourist season (December-February). Many tours fill up quickly!
